The Gidgets is the third band playing under our label. It is a surf band that consists entirely of female band members. In the meantime, this surf band has been bringing surf music from the 60s back to life for 5 years. The Gidgets has been present at many retro festivals all over Europe for several years and the band is gaining huge popularity every year. The band originated from four women who threw themselves into a world in which mainly the men made music. Still, they where not influenced by this and have grown into a well-known band.
